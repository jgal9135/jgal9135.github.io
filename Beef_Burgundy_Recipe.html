Easy Beef Burgundy

Ingredients

3 lbs. Chuck roast or beef stew

1 envelope dried onion soup

1 can cream of celery soup

1/2 teaspoon baking powder

1 cup Burgundy wine

Photo courtesy of Betty Crocker

Servings: 6

Active time: 10 mins

Total Time: 3 hours 10 mins
